Hello and welcome to my ass [actually this website]

I built this website in order to visualize graphs, that find shortest paths and sorting algorithms.

Besides visusaizing them, i also wanted to provide a quick introcution into how they work.

Of course, in order to explain how shortest-path algorithms work i also wrote a quick introduction into graph theory.

This introduction however is very basic and short, just to explain the simplest termns

Finally, i also want to cover the basics of runtime-analysis, so that the user can assume the speed of the algorithms, when applied on a large scale
